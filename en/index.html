NeoCubeGames Co., Ltd. Personal Information Management Policy

NeoCubeGames Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") protects users' personal information in accordance with relevant laws and regulations regarding personal information protection that the Company must comply with, such as the "Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection" and the "Personal Information Protection Act."

Through this Personal Information Management Policy, users can understand what personal information the Company collects, how it is used, to whom it is provided, and when and how it is disposed of.

The Company discloses this Personal Information Management Policy on the initial screen of its services, websites, customer centers, etc. so that users can easily access it.

If the Company revises its Personal Information Management Policy, it will notify users through website notices (or individual notices).

Collection of Personal Information and Collection Methods

1. Items of Personal Information Collected

The following information is collected when registering as a member:

  • For Facebook account usage: Member identification information
  • For Google account usage: Member identification information
  • For Apple account usage: Member identification information

* If you use the game with Facebook, Google, or Apple ID, only the member identification number provided by each social network, not other information, is collected/stored.

2. Methods of Collecting Personal Information

  • Collected through the sign-up and other procedures for joining the Company's services.
  • Collected separately through consent procedures for events and promotions.
  • Automatically collected through platforms that have partnership relationships with the Company regarding service provision.
  • Collected upon the user's voluntary provision or upon request if needed, during payment and user support during use.

Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

The Company does not disclose any personal information collected without the prior consent of its members. Collected personal information is not used for any purposes other than the following:

  • Contract performance related to service provision and settlement of charges according to service provision, provision of content, purchase and payment of fees, refunds, event winner notifications, shipment of goods and invoices, identity verification, and fee collection.
  • Member management, identity confirmation, personal identification, prevention of improper use by problematic members and unauthorized use, and more.
  • Development of new services and marketing, statistical analysis, demographic characteristics analysis, and validation of service effectiveness.

Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

The Company retains and uses personal information collected with the consent of members for as long as their membership remains valid. In principle, once the purpose of collecting and using personal information is achieved, the information is immediately deleted. However, certain information may be retained according to related laws and regulations.

Procedure and Method for Deleting Personal Information

1. Deletion Procedure

Information is stored for a certain period after the purpose is achieved and then deleted according to internal policies and other related laws regarding information protection.

2. Deletion Method

For personal information stored in electronic file form, technical methods are used for deletion. For printed personal information, it is shredded or incinerated.

Handling and Transferring Personal Information Outsourcing and Overseas

The Company outsources the handling of personal information to external professional agencies. Outsourced personal information is transferred via network when the service is used.

1. Outsourced Targets and Work

Amazon Web Services, Inc.

Purpose of Transfer: Storing information in cloud storage space.

Google LLC

Purpose of Transfer: Storing information in cloud storage space.

Rights of Users and Legal Guardians

Users and legal guardians may inquire or modify their own personal information or the personal information of the relevant minor at any time, and may also request withdrawal of membership.
